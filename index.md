------------------------------------------------------------------------

R package for crosswalking measures of cognition.

Methods described in:

Ackley SF, Wang J, Chen R, Hill-Jarrett TG, Rojas-Saunero LP, Stokes A,
Shah SJ, and Glymour MM for the Alzheimer’s Disease Neuroimaging
Initiative. **Methods to crosswalk between cognitive test scores using
data from the Alzheimer’s Disease Neuroimaging Cohort**. *Alzheimer’s &
Dementia*. 2025;21(2). doi:
[10.1002/alz.14597](https://doi.org/10.1002/alz.14597)

Funded by grants from the National Institute on Aging (R00AG073454 and
P01AG082653).

`cogxwalkr` is under active development. Please submit bug reports and
feature requests via
[Issues](https://github.com/jrgant/cogxwalkr/issues).
